Tuesday Men's Club

    This isn't group therapy and there's nothing to fix. We come together to hear one another's real experiences. We like connection, compassion, and presence. We end casually with ice cream and conversation.

    When & Where

    Every other Tuesday
    7:00pm - 8:30pm

    Wende Museum
    10808 Culver Blvd, Culver City, CA
    Use parking lot entrance

    What It Is

      Tuesday Men's Club welcomes men of all ages and backgrounds into a space for honest connection, reflection, and conversation. We begin with introductions and a prompt, followed by a short guided meditationâ€”often centered on breath or loving-kindness.

      From there, we move into lightly facilitated group discussion. Topics have included making friends, being a parent, finding joy in our everyday, and how solitude works. Also sometimes we talk football, work, and boogie boarding.

      How We Do It

      Each session follows a simple structure:

      • Introductions - Brief check-ins with a simple prompt
      • Meditation - 15-20 minutes of guided meditation, often loving-kindness practice
      • Discussion - Open conversation about topics like grief, fatherhood, friendship, aging, and everyday emotions
      • Ice cream - Casual conversation to close

      First Time?

      Drop-ins are welcome. No experience with meditation or group discussion necessary.

      We speak from personal experience, listen without giving advice, and keep conversations confidential.
