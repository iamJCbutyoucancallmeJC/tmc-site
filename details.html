What to Expect

Each session follows a simple structure:

  • Introductions - Brief check-ins with a simple prompt
  • Meditation - 15-20 minutes of guided practice, often loving-kindness
  • Discussion - Open conversation about topics like friendship, fatherhood, aging, and everyday emotions
  • Ice cream - Casual conversation to close

First Time?

Drop-ins are welcome. No experience with meditation or group discussion necessary.

We speak from personal experience, listen without giving advice, and keep conversations confidential.
